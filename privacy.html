Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025 Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you:

  • Book grooming services through our website
  • Contact us via email or phone

This may include your name, email address, phone number, pet details, and payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and manage your appointments
  • Communicate about your bookings
  • Improve our services
  • Send promotional offers (if you opt-in)

3. Data Protection

We implement security measures including:

  • Secure transmission via SSL encryption
  • Limited staff access to personal data
  • Regular system security updates

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Forms for bookings which operates under Google's privacy policy. We don't sell or share your data with other third parties except as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal data (subject to legal requirements)

Contact us at snazzypaws@co.nz for any privacy-related requests.
